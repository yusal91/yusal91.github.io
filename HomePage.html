Welcome to my portfolio page

My name is Juma Salman Ali, and I graduated from Future Game in 2022. I've had a passion for game development since childhood. After finishing college, I noticed that there weren't many opportunities in the field. When I came across the Future Games advertisement, I eagerly applied. Receiving my acceptance letter filled me with excitement and joy. I loved every class and found myself wanting to learn more and more with each session.

In my free time, I enjoy playing games that have engaging stories, and my top choice is role-playing games. Looking ahead, I aspire to create something wonderful, similar to the RPGs I loved playing. As for my personality, friends and acquaintances often see me as friendly, courteous, loyal, and quite self-disciplined, whether in person or online.
