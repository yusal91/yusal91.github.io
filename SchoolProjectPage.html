Here is the project I worked on during school time

Tower of Hanoi

This was our first project, and we had a specific theme to stick to. We aimed to create a puzzle game called "Tower of Hanoi." Even though we tried to follow the theme, we ran into some major resource issues. In the end, we went with a pirate character who explores and solves puzzles to find treasure. Since this was my first project and I was still learning the ropes of programming, I struggled a bit to put everything together. As a result, we ended up with a character that could push objects around to get through levels and reach the finish line.

Animal Treasure Quest

A multiplayer puzzle game played from a third-person perspective. We had an awesome team of designers and a super skilled programmer, along with myself. Everyone involved was really talented and contributed a lot. Unfortunately, my programmer fell ill and couldn’t wrap up the project, so we all did our best to bring it to completion.

Coin Collecting Mystery

My teacher gave me this assignment and suggested I tackle it on my own. He also recommended that I set up some time with him during our mentorship sessions for extra help. I’d get plenty of support to make sure I could add some playable content. The main goal was to find all the coins. Plus, I learned how to build a teleportation system between two platforms.
